ScienceRover

Welcome to ScienceRover, a Multi-Agent System for Scientific Discovery. This application uses artificial intelligence to generate research hypotheses based on the concepts you provide. Our system employs multiple AI agents to analyze relationships between concepts, generate hypotheses, and provide critical reviews.

How to use:

  1. Enter two or more concepts separated by commas in the input field below.
  2. Click the "Generate Hypothesis" button to start the process.
  3. Wait for the system to analyze the concepts and generate results.
  4. Review the output from the Graph Analysis, Ontologist, Scientist, and Critic agents.
